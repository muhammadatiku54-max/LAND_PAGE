How to do it:

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based business model where you earn a commission by promoting someone else's products or services. Essentially, you act as a digital salesperson for a brand: you find a product you like, promote it to others using a unique tracking link, and earn a piece of the profit for each sale or lead you generate. The merchant handles the inventory, shipping, and customer support, while your primary job is to drive traffic and build trust with your audience.

Wealth Generation:

To generate money from affiliate marketing, you first need to choose a niche (a specific topic like fitness, tech, or personal finance) and a platform (such as a blog, YouTube channel, or Instagram account) where you can share content. You then join an affiliate program, like Amazon Associates or ShareASale, to get your unique links.
